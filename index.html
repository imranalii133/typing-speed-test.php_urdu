How are the Urdu words from the test picked?

We have decided to use 1,500 most common words from the Urdu language. You can type in your own Urdu words or Urdu article if you want. We have put these options in Custom Urdu Text for you. You can think of it as a touch of email flavour to make the typing Urdu test a bit more interesting. The Urdu typing speed test focuses on typing itself, thus words appear randomly. Reading full sentences may influence your Urdu typing speed.


How many Urdu words can you type per minute?

The average person types between 38 and 40 Urdu words per minute (WPM), what Urdu translates into between 190 and 200 characters per minute (CPM). However, professional typists type a lot faster — on average between 65 and 75 WPM Urdu word.

Why Urdu typing speed matters?

The faster you can Urdu type, the faster you will be able to communicate with others on Urdu language. You will be able to save a ton of time on any kind of work that requires Urdu typing. At first, it will be a couple of extra minutes that you won’t really notice. Over time, the minutes will change to the hours you can only do in other own activities.


What is the average Urdu typing speed?

An average professional Urdu typist types usually in speed of 65 to 75 WPM on Urdu language. More advanced positions require 80 to 95 Urdu word for Urdu language. There are also some advanced Urdu typists work that requires speeds above 120 WPM Urdu language.
